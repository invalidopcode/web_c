Welcome to the coMUTINY example site. coMUTINY is a blogging and discussion platform built for community campaigns. Inspired by Bristol co-mutiny

Technical Details

  • fully free software
  • reliable, scalable, client-microserver architecture
  • privacy friendly - no tracking, no cookies unless you sign in, minimal data stored
  • no google! Zero use of google fonts, etc, in the entire stack. No use of any services controlled by Google, Apple, Amazon, or Microsoft
  • server-side uses the Kore platform; client-side uses emscripten-compiled javascript
